---
name: "Planning Output"
description: "Planning output must be decomposed into .firm/ artifacts"
owner: "The Firm Architecture Team"
language: always
---

All planning output must be captured as structured .firm/ artifacts.
Decisions go to `.firm/concepts/decisions/` with proper frontmatter.
Patterns go to `.firm/concepts/patterns/` with problem/solution structure.
Standards go to `.firm/lookup/standards/` with rationale and enforcement.
Guides go to `.firm/guides/workflows/` with steps and verification.
Raw text dumps are prohibited — every output must follow a template.
